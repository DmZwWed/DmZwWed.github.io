Venue

How do I get to the venue?
  1. There is a dropoff point at Sunway Resort Hotel & Spa.
  2. After the entrance, turn left and you can see a narrow pathway leading to The Resort Café @ Sunway Resort.
  3. Follow the path and you can see a lift and escalator, the ballroom is two floors above you.
Where should guests park?
  1. Drop off at Sunway Resort Hotel & Spa, there is an indoor parking sign following the driveway.
  2. Resort Hotel Outdoor Parking.
    (You can drop guests off, then drive here)
  3. Sunway Pyramid Shopping Mall.
    (Roughly 10 minutes walking through the mall)

Note: All Sunway Hotels and Malls have the same parking rate.

How to pay for parking after entering without a ticket?
  1. Pals For Life App.
  2. Mobile Payment.
  3. Use Touch 'N Go Card at the exit.
