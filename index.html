Where is the wedding reception (dinner)?

Hall 1 & 2 of Grand Lagoon Ballroom, at Sunway Pyramid Hotel.

How do I get to the venue?

There is a dropoff point at Sunway Pyramid Hotel.
After the entrance, turn left and you can see a narrow pathway leading to a restaurant.
Follow the path and you can see a lift and escalator, the ballroom is two floors above you.

What time should I arrive?

6 PM - Cocktail
7 PM - Reception.

Where should guests park?

  1. Drop off at Sunway Pyramid Hotel, there is an indoor parking sign following the driveway.
  2. Resort Hotel Outdoor Parking (You can drop guests off, then drive here).
  3. Sunway Pyramid Shopping Mall (Roughly 10 minutes walking through the mall.)
  4. *Note: All Sunway Hotels and Malls have the same parking rate.
